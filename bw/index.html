About me

Photographer / Graphic Designer.

My name is Derek Do and I am a fashion and beauty photographer based in Boston, USA. I was born in a small city in Vietnam and developed an early love for art since the very early days. After devoting my teenage years to drawing and graphic design, I developed a passion for photography that I was decided to grow into a career.

I’ve always been in love with every single thing that involves fashion. In my work I try to follow an attractive and minimalist line, being consistent with my own style.
